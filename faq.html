Industrial Automation

We support automation across 11 industries including semiconductor manufacturing, automotive production, machine tools, food processing, electronics manufacturing, packaging, drone technology, research institutes, education, government projects, and SMEs. Our automation solutions are tailored to each industry's specific requirements and compliance standards.

Our optimization approach follows a systematic 8-phase process: Consultation → Site Investigation → Engineering Analysis → Concept Design → Simulation → Manufacturing Support → Implementation → Continuous Improvement. We begin with value stream mapping and OEE analysis to identify bottlenecks, then apply lean manufacturing principles and digital twin simulation to redesign workflows for maximum throughput.

Typical ROI for industrial automation projects ranges from 20% to 40% within the first 18 months, depending on project scope and industry. Our clients usually achieve measurable improvements within 6–9 months of implementation through reduced labor costs, decreased energy consumption, improved quality metrics, and increased production throughput.

Engineering Process

We adhere to CE Machinery Directive (2006/42/EC), DIN standards (DIN EN ISO, DIN EN 61355, DIN ISO 128), ISO 9001 quality management, ISO 12100 risk assessment, ISO 13849-1/IEC 62061 functional safety, and 5S lean manufacturing methodology. All deliverables are documented per German engineering tradition for traceability and audit readiness.

Project timelines vary by scope and complexity. A typical industrial automation consulting project takes 3–6 months from initial consultation to implementation. Full engineering design projects usually require 6–12 months. We provide detailed project timelines during the quotation phase with clear milestones and deliverable schedules.
