DerMech Engineering Team

German Engineer | Automation Consultant | Software Developer | Engineering Educator

DerMech Solution is led by a German mechanical engineering team with over 20 years of combined experience leading industrial automation and engineering design projects across multinational corporations in Germany, Taiwan, and Greater China. Our team's expertise spans the full spectrum of industrial engineering — from factory automation and production line optimization to mechanical design, software development, and technical education.

After completing their mechanical engineering studies in Germany, our team members held senior engineering positions at leading industrial companies where they spearheaded factory modernization programs, implemented Industry 4.0 initiatives, and developed cross-disciplinary engineering standards. Our team's work has directly contributed to significant efficiency gains, energy reductions, and quality improvements across automotive, semiconductor, and machinery sectors.

In 2024, DerMech Solution was founded to bridge German engineering rigor with the dynamic manufacturing landscape of Asia. Our vision: deliver end-to-end engineering consulting that combines CE/DIN standards compliance with practical, implementation-ready solutions tailored to each client's unique challenges.

Beyond consulting, our team is passionate about engineering education. We have mentored numerous students in STEM programs, coached national competition teams in Germany, and advocate for project-based learning that connects theoretical knowledge with real-world engineering practice.

EDUCATION & MENTORING

Engineering the Next Generation

Our team believes that engineering excellence is not just about technical competence — it's about cultivating a mindset of curiosity, rigor, and continuous improvement. Through DerMech Solution's education initiatives, we bring this philosophy to students, professionals, and organizations.

Our team's experience includes coaching national-level engineering competition teams in Germany, designing STEM curricula for secondary and tertiary education, and delivering hands-on workshops in mechanical design, Arduino/Python programming, and project-based engineering. These programs emphasize practical problem-solving over theoretical memorization, preparing participants for real-world engineering challenges.

Whether mentoring a student's first Arduino project or guiding a professional team through a complex automation deployment, the approach remains the same: German engineering discipline, applied with precision and purpose.
